25 Best iPhone Tips and Tricks to Improve Your Daily Experience (2026 Guide)

Discover the best iPhone tips and tricks that can help you improve battery life, boost security, increase productivity, and get the most out of your iPhone. This beginner-friendly guide covers everything from hidden features to advanced settings.

Apple's iPhone is packed with powerful features that many users never discover. Whether you've just purchased your first iPhone or have been using one for years, learning a few hidden settings and smart tricks can dramatically improve your experience.

In this guide, you'll learn the best iPhone tips that help you save battery, increase privacy, improve security, take better photos, and work more efficiently.

1. Keep iOS Updated

Apple regularly releases updates that improve security, performance, battery optimization, and new features. Always install the latest version of iOS to keep your device running smoothly.

2. Enable Face ID Security

Face ID offers one of the most secure smartphone authentication systems. Make sure it's enabled for unlocking your phone, banking apps, passwords, and App Store purchases.

3. Optimize Battery Health

Go to:

Settings → Battery → Battery Health

Enable Optimized Battery Charging to slow battery aging and increase the lifespan of your iPhone.

4. Use Low Power Mode

When your battery is running low, enable Low Power Mode to reduce background activity and extend battery life.

5. Customize Control Center

Add shortcuts for:

  • Flashlight

  • Calculator

  • Screen Recording

  • Camera

  • QR Code Scanner

  • Voice Memos

This saves time and improves accessibility.

6. Organize Your Home Screen

Create folders for apps like:

  • Work

  • Social Media

  • Finance

  • Travel

  • Photography

You can also hide unnecessary pages for a cleaner experience.

7. Use Focus Modes

Focus Mode helps reduce distractions while working, sleeping, driving, or studying by limiting notifications and customizing home screens.

8. Master Widgets

Widgets provide quick information without opening apps.

Useful widgets include:

  • Weather

  • Calendar

  • Notes

  • Battery

  • Fitness

  • Reminders

9. Improve Camera Quality

For better photos:

  • Enable Grid

  • Use Portrait Mode

  • Turn on HDR

  • Shoot in ProRAW (supported models)

  • Lock Exposure

  • Use Night Mode

These settings significantly improve image quality.

10. Scan Documents with Notes

Open the Notes app and use the built-in document scanner to create high-quality PDFs without installing additional apps.

11. Use Live Text

Live Text lets you copy text directly from images, signs, documents, and screenshots.

It's perfect for students, professionals, and travelers.

12. Hide Private Photos

Move sensitive images into the Hidden Album and protect them using Face ID for extra privacy.

13. Enable Find My iPhone

If your device is lost, Find My can locate, lock, or erase it remotely.

Always keep this feature enabled.

14. Use iCloud Backup

Automatic backups ensure your photos, contacts, messages, and settings are protected.

Schedule regular backups over Wi-Fi.

15. Manage Storage

Navigate to:

Settings → General → iPhone Storage

Delete unused apps, large videos, and duplicate photos to free up storage space.

16. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Protect your Apple Account with two-factor authentication to prevent unauthorized access.

17. Customize Your Lock Screen

You can personalize:

  • Fonts

  • Wallpapers

  • Widgets

  • Clock styles

  • Multiple lock screens

This makes your iPhone both stylish and functional.

18. Use Siri Shortcuts

Automate everyday tasks like:

  • Sending messages

  • Playing playlists

  • Opening favorite apps

  • Starting workouts

  • Controlling smart home devices

19. Improve Safari Privacy

Safari includes excellent privacy features.

Enable:

  • Prevent Cross-Site Tracking

  • Hide IP Address

  • Fraudulent Website Warning

These settings help protect your online activity.

20. Share Wi-Fi Passwords

Apple allows nearby devices signed into Apple Accounts to securely share Wi-Fi passwords without typing them manually.

21. Translate Text Instantly

The built-in Translate app supports multiple languages and can translate text, conversations, and even downloaded offline languages.

22. Record Your Screen

Use Screen Recording from Control Center to capture tutorials, gameplay, or troubleshooting videos with ease.

23. Use Emergency SOS

Emergency SOS quickly contacts local emergency services and notifies your emergency contacts if needed.

Configure it in your Safety settings.

24. Hidden Trackpad Feature

Press and hold the space bar to turn your keyboard into a trackpad, making text editing much easier.

25. Keyboard Shortcuts

Save time by using features like:

  • Text Replacement

  • Swipe Typing

  • Undo by shaking the device (where supported)

  • Quick punctuation

  • Clipboard shortcuts

These small tricks can greatly improve typing speed.

Final Thoughts

Your iPhone is capable of much more than making calls and sending messages. By using these hidden features and smart settings, you can improve productivity, strengthen security, extend battery life, and enjoy a smoother overall experience.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced iPhone user, applying these tips will help you get the most from your device every day.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ Schema Ready)

What are the best iPhone tips for beginners?

Beginners should start by enabling Face ID, setting up iCloud Backup, using Focus Mode, customizing Control Center, and enabling Find My iPhone.

How can I improve my iPhone battery life?

Enable Optimized Battery Charging, reduce screen brightness, use Low Power Mode, disable unnecessary background app refresh, and keep iOS updated.

Is Face ID secure?

Yes. Face ID is one of the most secure biometric authentication systems available and is designed to protect your personal information.

How do I free up storage on my iPhone?

Delete unused apps, remove duplicate photos, clear downloaded files, and enable iCloud Photos to optimize storage.

What is Live Text on iPhone?

Live Text allows you to copy, translate, and interact with text found in photos and screenshots.
